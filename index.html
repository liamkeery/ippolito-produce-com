What We Do

Commodities

From our growing partners across North America, including our sister company Ippolito International, we source what matters to you. Local when we can, Organic when you want it, but always in season and great value.

Value Add

Our facilities in Burlington, Ontario wash, process and pack over 12,000,000 lbs of leafy greens each year. We also dice, chop, and shred certain vegetables. We deliver fresh value to our customer base in Canada and the United States.

Specialty Services

Service is at the heart of everything we do. From inbound quality inspections, to icing services & reclamation, from consolidating loads to regrading & repacking, bagging, ripening, merchandising support & menu development – we are flexible enough to be your extended produce arm.

Value-Added Produce

At Ippolito Fruit & Produce our in-house processing offers a wide variety of value-add options. We process and pack over 12 million pounds of leafy greens annually. We wash and process single leaf items, such as Arugula, Spinach (baby, teen, savoy/curly), Baby Kale and chopped Tuscan Kale, as well as a variety of blends, including 50/50 mix, spring mix and harvest blends. We can even customize a mix based on your specifications. We also peel and dice over 300,000 pounds of squash and tubers per year at our Burlington, Ontario facility, which can be packed to suit your needs, 20lb bulk or individual trays ready for retail.

Organics

Caring how & where your food is produced is important to you. At Ippolito Fruit & Produce we are proud to offer our certified organic line of products under our Lovin’ Life and Queen Victoria Organic brands featuring Arugula, Baby Spinach, Kale, Spring Mix, 50/50 blends and more.

Our supply chain team sources leafy greens from across North America that will meet our strict organic growing standards, and our Sister company Ippolito International has been growing Certified Organic Commodities like Asparagus, Broccoli, Celery, Cilantro and now Brussel Sprouts adding more options every year.
