What We Do

Commodities

From our growing partners across North America, including our sister company Ippolito International, we source what matters to you. Local when we can, Organic when you want it, but always in season and great value.

Value Add

Our facilities in Burlington, Ontario wash, process and pack over 10,000,000 Lbs of leafy greens each year – delivering fresh value to our customer base in Canada and the United States.

Specialty Services

Service is at the heart of everything we do. From inbound quality inspections, to icing services and reclamation, from consolidating loads to regrading and repacking, bagging, ripening, merchandising support and menu development – we are flexible enough to be your extended produce arm.

Explore The Options

Organic Value Add

From the fields our products are grown pesticide free, they are then washed using an organic rinse agent before being dried and packaged on our lines – our process is certified by both the USDA and CFIA.

Conventional Value Add

From our growing partners across North America – including our sister company Ippolito International – we harvest the best available product and transport using Ippolito Transportation to our Burlington, Ontario Facility where we wash, process and pack our products into a variety of pack sizes, from clam shells to cello bags raging in size from 5oz to 5lbs we can customize a program for you. From washed and cleaned Arugula, to Diced Yams we can do it all, with the same care and concern you would serve to your families.

Commodity Program

Local when in season, organic when you want it always the right value, using our network of over 30 growers, including our sister company Ippolito International we work to find our customers the right solution for their produce needs. All of our products are fully traceable and comply with federal standards. We closely monitor our growing partners food safety audits and ensure they are following industry standard GAPs.
