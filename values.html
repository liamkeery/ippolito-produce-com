What We Value

We are proud of the role we play in the food supply chain, providing safe, healthy, quality produce to our families, neighbors, and friends. As a Canadian family-owned business, we have some core values and beliefs that inspire us and underpin our dealings internally and externally. We strive for a culture that is an expression of these values and beliefs, and they provide the lens through which we examine our own actions, and those of our partners:

We are Safety Driven  – Nobody should get hurt earning a livelihood. Nobody should get sick eating food they buy.

We value Family  – We do not stray far from our roots.

We are Transparent  – What you see is what you get.

We are Flexible  – With operations in their backyards we can pivot with our customers’ needs.

We are Accountable  – We say what we do, and we do what we say.

We are aware of our Environmental Footprint  – And we strive to reduce it.

We support our Community  – We are good neighbors and citizens.

We nourish a thank-you culture  – We are grateful for our teammates.
