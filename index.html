What We Do

Commodities

From our growing partners across North America, including our sister company Ippolito International, we source what matters to you. Local when we can, Organic when you want it, but always in season and great value.

Value Add

Our facilities in Burlington, Ontario wash, process and pack over 10,000,000 lbs of leafy greens each year – delivering fresh value to our customer base in Canada and the United States.

Specialty Services

Service is at the heart of everything we do. From inbound quality inspections, to icing services and reclamation, from consolidating loads to regrading and repacking, bagging, ripening, merchandising support and menu development – we are flexible enough to be your extended produce arm.
